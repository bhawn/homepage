Even More About Me

 As I mentioned earlier, I am a recent graduate of Texas A&Ms Master of Computer Science program. Although a little strange, I first got started into the subject back when I was 8 years old playing a web-based game called neopets. In this game everyone had their own homepage which you could customize using HTML which is where I was first introduced into any sort of programming. Although at the time I didn't learn much, it still started my interest into the field. From this initial interest, years later I would take high-school computer science classes, go to programming summer camp, graduate from Texas A&M with my bachelor of science in computer science, and finally graduate from Texas A&M with my Master of Computer Science degree.
